# Sphere

Spherical geometry.

## Center

`center`: Center of object in x, y, and z.

Type: floating-point number

-   Unit: $\mu$m
-   Default: (*0*, *0*, *0*)

## Radius

`radius`: Radius of geometry.

Type: floating-point number

-   Unit: $\mu$m
-   Constraint: greater than or equal to *0*
-   Required field
