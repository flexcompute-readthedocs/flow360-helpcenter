# BoundarySpec

Specifies boundary conditions on each side of the domain and along each dimension.

## Boundary Condition Along X, Y, and Z Axes

Boundary condition on the plus (*+*) and minus (*-*) sides along the each axis.

Options:

-   [Periodic](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/Periodic.html)
-   [PECBoundary](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/PECBoundary.html)
-   [PMCBoundary](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/PMCBoundary.html)
-   [PML](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/PML.html)
-   [StablePML](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/StablePML.html)
-   [Absorber](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/Absorber.html)
-   [BlochBoundary](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/BlochBoundary.html)

Default: [Periodic](/07.boundary/02.boundary%20type/Periodic.html)
